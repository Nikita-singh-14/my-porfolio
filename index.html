E-Waste Management

E-waste is one of the fastest growing waste and it needs to be focused and addressed. Only 5 percent of E-waste has been recycling across the world.The website "E-waste Collection Service Using Web Portal" will have a user-friendly interface and it contains option to upload snapshot of the E-waste collected and it prompts the user to provide the location. With these details we approach the user after sending confirmation message to user's registered mobile number. After collecting the E-waste from the user, we handover the collected waste to the nearest E-waste collection organisation and through our website we credit the amount directly to his/her bank account based upon the amount of E-waste collected.
